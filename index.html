Biography

I am currenlty a research scientist at Algorand. I am going to join UC Santa Barbara as an assistant professor in Fall 2020.

I graudated as a PhD from University of Washington Computer Science and Engineering. I was advised by the great Dan Suciu. I mostly worked on Cosette, an automated reasoning tool for SQL and Myria, a distributed database system. I obtained my bachelor degree from Wuhan University, China, which has one of the most beautiful campus in the world.

What’s up?

  • I am actively recruiting PhD/Postdoc. Please apply to UCSB and mention my name if you are interested in (database) systems, formal methods, and applied crypto. see my research agenda here. Also, I am happy to chat in general if you drop me an email.
  • Our analysis of blockchain system preprint is online!
